About.
3D-renderer
When the 3D-renderer in the p5-library broke down, I saw it as a great oppurtunity to write one of my own and at the same time explore the as fascinating as it is challenging world of 3D graphics rendering. I went on to write some music for the scene and turned the surroundings into a visualizer of sorts with the help of the p5-sound library.
https://hilmerx3d.surge.sh
PacXon
A HTML5 remake of sorts and my own take on the great little arcade game PacXon. In terms of sheer size and challenge it is one of my most ambitious projects to date. Both in reasoning about fundamental game engine concepts as well as how to implement physics and use alorithms to make the game actually come alive.
https://pacxon.surge.sh
Guest Book Tunnel
An entrance installation at the Hyper Island "Exploring Tech"-event. The concept was for the visitor to record their heart beat and temperature, using a heartbeat and a heat sensor, and then have that pulse and heat information recorded on the wall of lights by a unique blinking colored lamp. We ended up building a tunnel with 400 LED-lights on each side, resulting in a sea of pulses and colors.
https://vimeo.com/210244611
Music Makers
A website for my music productions. I also wrote a light CMS for easy updates. Written with an attempt at best practices working with mobile first, BEM, PostCSS, Gulp, responsive images, NPM, babel and webpacks and all that other good stuff!
http://www.musicmakers.se
Mario Level Maker
A level editor for a HTML5 version of Super Mario Bros 1, perhaps a poor mans Mario Maker if you like. During a course in remaking Super Mario for the web I saw the need to easily create levels, so I challenged myself to build an graphical interface and eventually to export appropriately formatted JSONs. It's a very recent project and a work in progress but one I am very excited about.
http://mario-level-maker.surge.sh/
